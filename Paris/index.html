    • A weekend on the seine

    I was lucky enough to spend three days visitnig Paris this month! Iwent with a friend and we were able to see many things while we were there!

    Day 1

    On our first day, we started by visiting Monmartre right away. Our second stop was the Eiffel Tower (check out that beautiful picture!) We ate dinner at a cute little bistro overlooking la Seine

    Day 2

    On our second day, we went shopping on Avenue des champs Elysees. Every single person we saw was dressed in the height of fastion



    After we finished shopping, we visited some really beautiful place! We went to Notre Dame. We went on a macaron tour of Paris and ate all of the best macarons the city had to offer. After we finished shopping, we visited le louvre

    Day 3

    The picture below is of me visiting the Arc de Triomphe. It's huge! We also went to musee D'Orsay. seeing the work of Renoir, Monet, and Van Gogh in person was completely breathtaking. I was surprised to see that the museum also had many scuptures including those by Auguste Rodin, Paul Gaugain, and Alfred Barye
